Customer Service, Pricing and other Information

Membership Costs
Lawrence Creates is very inexpensive. Membership costs just $20 per month, with a $20 initiation fee. There is no contract, and membership can be cancelled at any time. Our Membership and Cancellation policy is below.

Customer Service Contact Information
Lawrence Creates members or those enrolled in training classes may contact Eric Kirkendall for customer service. Eric may be contacted by email at kirkendall1@gmail.com, by phone at 785-260-0083, or in person at the Lawrence Creates Makerspace, 512, E. 9th Street, Lawrence KS 66044

Hours of Operation
Members are provided access and are able to use the Lawrence Creates Makerspace any time. Public hours, during which prospective members can tour the facility and enroll, are posted at

Membership Fee and Cancellation Policy
Members can pay their initiation and monthly fees on-site, if they wish. However, to keep membership costs low, we ask that members pay their monthly fee by credit card, debit card, or Paypal if possible; and pay only their one-time initiation fee onsite.
The monthly membership fee is automatically charged to a memberâ€™s Paypal account or credit card for the initial and recurring months.
Members can cancel their membership, anytime, effective on the day the next membership fee is due. For example, if a member joins on the 16th of January, they will be billed $20 on that date, and on the 16th of February and each successive month, and can cancel anytime. If this member cancels their membership, membership cancellation will be effective on the 16th of the current or next month, whichever comes first.

Training class cancellation policy
Training class fees are non-refundable. However, those who cancel training classes by sending an email message to kirkendall1@gmail.com at least 48 hours in advance of the class may take the same class within six months at no cost, subject to class availability.

Item Return Policy
Merchandise (e.g., art) sales are non-refundable.

Privacy Policy
We do not share marketing or other information on members or anyone else we do business with.
