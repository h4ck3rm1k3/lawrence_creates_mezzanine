Membership

If you haven't yet joined the Lawrence Creates Makerspace, please join today.  Our grassroots, community-focused economic development organization is in the startup phase, and your support is very important.


The monthly fee is just $20, which you can pay onsite or on our website (under the Join Link) by credit card, plus a one-time $20 initiation fee, which you can pay at the Makerspace when you visit.  There is no commitment, members can quit any time.

Members are given a key, and can come anytime. If you have joined, but not yet gotten a key, please come by and pick one up.   

If you are not yet a member, you are welcome to visit.  Our public hours are now posted on our website (select the calendar link). Just click the link, and then "Calendar".  Please come check us out, and tell us how we can help you or others develop skills. create a business, or enhance a life.

 Please join today. Your modest financial support will allow us to make the Lawrence Creates Makerspace sustainable, so we can continue to serve the community.

 Thank you!
